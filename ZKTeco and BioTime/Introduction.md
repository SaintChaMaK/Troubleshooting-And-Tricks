#### ZKTeco

**ZKTeco Co., Ltd.** is a globally renowned enterprise specializing in biometric verification, access control, and intelligent security solutions.  Founded in March 1998, ZKTeco is a world leader in hybrid biometric identification technology, offering advanced solutions in fingerprint, facial recognition, iris, and palm vein recognition. The company provides comprehensive services across three core areas: **Biometric Verification Core Technique Empowerment**, **Smart Entrance & Exit “Person + Car + Object” Software Platform Empowerment**, and **ZKTeco+ Smart Office Cloud Platform Empowerment**. 

ZKTeco serves public services, enterprise-level users, and personal users worldwide, with operations in over 100 countries and regions. It owns the largest number of patents and intellectual property rights in biometric technology and integrates AI, IoT, cloud computing, and big data into its products. Its solutions are used in sectors including smart government, finance, education, healthcare, traffic, and retail.


#### BioTime

**BioTime** is ZKTeco’s web-based **time and attendance management software**, designed for enterprise-level workforce automation.  It supports real-time synchronization with ZKTeco’s biometric devices (e.g., fingerprint, face, palm) via the **PUSH protocol** over Ethernet, Wi-Fi, or 4G. 

Key features include:

- **Shift scheduling, roster management, and overtime calculation**
    
- **Employee self-service** via mobile app (ZKBioTime) with geofencing for remote clock-in/out
    
- **Visitor management, access control, and meeting management**
    
- **API integration** with HR, ERP, and payroll systems
    
- **Real-time monitoring, automated reports, and email/WhatsApp notifications**
    
- Supports **PostgreSQL, MSSQL, MySQL, and Oracle** databases
    
- Compliant with **ISO/IEC 27001, ISO/IEC 27701, ISO9001, and ISO20000** certifications 
    

The mobile companion app, **ZKBioTime**, is available for **iOS and Android**, enabling employees to clock in, submit requests, and view attendance records remotely.
